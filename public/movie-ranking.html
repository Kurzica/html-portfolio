The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar

This is my favourite movie of all time. I love the scifi elements as well as how surprisingly emotional it is.

Oppenheimer

Nolan's magnum opus. Chilling biopic of Oppenheimer showing his greatest achievement and biggest struggle with everyone else downplaying the severity.

Kill Bill

Classic Tarantino love letter to Japanese film, Uma Thurman excellent as the main character in her titular quest to kill her scorned former lover.
